What partners get

Trusted relationships, not transactions

Trusted referral path A clear channel for your vetted founders to connect with VBF for potential investment consideration — on terms that respect the work you've already done.
Programming & co-delivery Curriculum, speaker access, and session hosting aligned with your program — we show up as operators, not distant capital.
Visibility across the network Shared presence with other partner programs in the VBF orbit — so good work gets seen by peers who care about the same problems.
Ecosystem reinvestment A portion of GP carry is directed back into partner ecosystems — aligning incentives with the organizations that source and develop founders.

Our partnership approach

Substantive relationships, built over time

We work with builders the same way we hope you work with founders: with patience, clarity, and a long horizon. Your cohorts get a direct line to VBF when investment fit is possible; you get ongoing programming and a partner who has spent years inside accelerator and studio operations — in many cases, we helped teach these organizations the practices they use today.

Peer operators

We are not parachuting in from the coasts. We show up as people who have built programs, trained ecosystem leaders, and earned trust in the field.

Aligned incentives

When your founders succeed, the ecosystem succeeds — and we structure our partnerships so value flows back to the organizations doing the hard screening and coaching work.
