How We Invest Differently

Most venture funds wait until startups have proven traction, competing with dozens of other investors for the same deals. This gives us first access to companies that have been rigorously vetted, operationally supported, and set up for success, but haven't yet hit the traditional VC radar.

Our approach combines the upside of early-stage investing with the risk reduction of institutional validation. We don't discover startups in the wild‚Äîwe partner with the organizations that build them.

Traditional VCs compete for the same deals at the same time. By the time a startup appears on their radar, valuations have climbed and competition is fierce. We invest months or even years earlier‚Äîwhen founders are still emerging from structured programs, before the feeding frenzy begins.

This timing advantage isn't just about lower valuations. It's about relationship primacy. When we invest, we become the founding investor‚Äîthe one founders remember, trust, and turn to first. That relationship compounds throughout the company's lifecycle, giving us access to follow-on rounds, insider information, and referrals to their founder networks.

The Venture Builder Ecosystem is Maturing

Over the past decade, the venture-building industry has professionalized. What started as scattered experiments in startup creation has evolved into a global infrastructure of proven programs with track records, methodologies, and alumni networks.

The numbers tell the story:

  • 1,000+ active accelerators and venture studios globally
  • $50B+ deployed through structured venture-building programs since 2010
  • 30%+ of unicorns went through accelerators or incubation programs
  • Accelerator-backed companies raise follow-on funding at 2-3x the rate of non-accelerated peers

But there's a gap: Most of these programs lack dedicated follow-on capital vehicles. Their graduates either struggle to raise traditional seed rounds or get picked off by opportunistic investors who didn't contribute to their development.

We fill that gap. By providing patient, founder-friendly capital to the best companies emerging from the best programs, we capture deal flow that traditional VCs miss‚Äîand we do it with structural advantages they can't replicate.

The ecosystem is mature. The infrastructure is proven. The timing is perfect.
