Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

We collect information you provide directly — such as your name, email address, company details, and pitch deck when you create a founder profile. We also collect basic analytics data about how visitors use our public website using Google Analytics 4.

Google Analytics

Our public website uses Google Analytics 4 to understand visitor behaviour (pages visited, time on site, general location by country). This data is anonymised and aggregated. We do not use it to identify individuals. You can decline analytics tracking via the cookie banner on our site, or install the Google Analytics opt-out browser extension.

Founder data

Information submitted through the founder profile form is stored securely in our database and shared only with verified investors inside our VentureNext investor community. We do not sell your data to third parties. You can request deletion of your data at any time by emailing hello@venturebuilders.fund.

Investor portal

The investor portal does not use third-party analytics. Activity within the portal (such as deck views) is logged internally for the purpose of notifying founders of investor interest.

Cookies

Our public website uses cookies for Google Analytics (only with your consent) and to remember your cookie preference. We do not use advertising cookies or sell data to advertisers.

Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data deletion requests: hello@venturebuilders.fund
